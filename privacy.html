Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled when you use the Personal FinanceTracker tool. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

The Personal FinanceTracker is designed to be a private and secure tool for your financial management. The core functionality is based on Google Sheets and Google Forms, which means your data is stored in your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to your individual financial data, which includes:

  • Your financial transactions (income, expenses, etc.)
  • Your personal financial statements
  • Any other specific financial data you enter into your copy of the Google Sheet.

How We Use Your Information

The only data we collect is your **email address**, which is required to grant you access to the master file of the FinanceTracker after purchase. This email is used solely for the purpose of setting up your access and providing direct support. We do not use your email for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Security
